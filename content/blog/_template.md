# Blog post template (content/blog/slug.md)

This file will list out the template format for all blog files. This template should be followed so content will display correctly on the website.

---

title: "Post Title Here"

slug: "post-slug-here"

date: YYYY-MM-DD

summary: "Short 1-2 sentence summary of what this post is about."

tags: [Examples]

- netxjs
- portfolio
- learning

relatedProjects:

- portfolio-app

status: Pending | In Progress | Complete | Published

---

## Context

Briefly explain **why** you worked on this.

- What problem were you trying to solve?
- Was this part of a larger project or learning goal?
- What triggered the work (bug, refactor, new feature, concept)?

Keep this short and concrete.

---

## What I Built / Changed

Describe **exactly** what you implemented.

- New feature, refactor, experiment, or setup
- Scope (samll / medium / exploratory)
- What files or areas were involved (high level)

Avoid marketing language. Be factual.

---

## Key Technical Decisions

Call out the **interesting or non-obvious choices**.

- Why this approach?
- What alternatives did you consider?
- Tradoffs you accepted (complexity, felxibility, performance)

This is where engineering judgement shows.

---

## Problems Encountered

Be honest and specific.

- What broke?
- What was confusing?
- What didn't work the first time?

Bullet points are fine?

---

## How I Solved Them

Explain the resolution clearly.

- What you changed
- What finally worked
- What you learned from the failure

Focus on *process*, not heroics.

---

## What I Learned

Summarize takeways.

- New concepts
- Reinforced fundamentals
- Things you'd do differently next time

Short, sharp bullets are ideal.

---

## Next Steps

List what comes next.

- Immediate follow-ups
- Deferred improvements
- Links to related issues or future posts

Optional, but very useful for showing momentum.

---

## References (Optional)

- Docs
- Articles
- Repos
- Issues

Only include if they genuinely add value.
